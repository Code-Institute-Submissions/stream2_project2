How It Works

About

Right Place is a common market place for product placement opportunities.

Either you're a marketer with a brand looking for a product placement opportunity or you're a content producer with a product placement opportunity looking for a brand.

Right place connects the two. Sign up to view opportunities and subscribe to connect.

Sign up

Once you have signed up marketers will be able to view opportunities posted by content producers and content producers will be able to view opportunities posted by marketers.

Subscribe

Subscriptions are offered on a monthly and annual basis.

You must be subscibed if you wish to connect with a opportunity. As a subscribed member, you will also be able to add your own opportunities and connect with interested parties.
